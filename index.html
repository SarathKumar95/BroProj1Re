Progress and Podiums for young
Japanese drivers in Lahti

Young Japanese drivers Yuki Yamamoto, Nao Otake
and Hikaru Kogure showed strong progress during
their second rallies as a part of the TOYOTA
GAZOO Racing WRC Challenge Program at the
Varaosaexpert Ralli: a national event held
alongside the Lahti Historic Rally in Finland.
Yamamoto finished on the overall podium in
third position (and second among two-wheel
drive entries) with Otake close behind in
fourth and Kogure in sixth.
